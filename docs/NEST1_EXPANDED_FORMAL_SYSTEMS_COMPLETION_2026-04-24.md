## Topology And Topography Split

The build should keep these separate:

| Term | Formal Role | Mirror Use |
| --- | --- | --- |
| topology | connectedness, holes, continuity, neighborhoods, deformation-stable structure | what remains connected or equivalent under lawful deformation |
| topography | surface shape, gradients, ridges, basins, peaks, local fields, spatial maps | where structure is located on a surface or field and how it slopes / concentrates |

Short rule:

topology asks whether the structure is still connected the same way.

topography asks where the structure sits and how the surface changes.
