## PFAS / Chemical Breakdown Lane

Core read:

PFAS breakdown is not scored by parent-compound disappearance alone.

The prototype lane must track:

- carbon-fluorine bond targets
- parent compound
- intermediate fragments
- short-chain PFAS descendants
- fluoride release
- total fluorine / mass balance
- toxicity or bioactivity reduction
- transfer into air / sludge / another medium

Score:

```text
remediation_score =
  parent_reduction
  + defluorination
  + safe_endpoint_evidence
  + mass_balance_closure
  - harmful_descendant_penalty
  - transfer_penalty
```
